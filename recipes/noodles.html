NIGERIAN JOLLOF NOODLES

DESCRIPTION

Jollof is as Nigerian as the colour green. It belongs to us. We combine real
Naija flavour with instant noodles for a delicious experience. Instant Fried Jollof is better
enjoyed with sliced sausage, chicken or turkey.

Ingredients

  • 2 Packs of Indomie noodles
  • 2 tsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 2 raw eggs
  • 1 tsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp of green pepper
  • Green cabbage
  • Sweet corn
  • Spring onions

Steps

  1. Boil 2 packs of Indomie noodles. Set aside. You can either leave it wholesome or crunch it depending on your preference.
  2. Beat up the eggs.
  3. 2 tsp Soya Oil. Fry the eggs until it scrambles. Set aside
